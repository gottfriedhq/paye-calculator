Included assumptions

PAYE is calculated on monthly chargeable employment income after the SSNIT deduction toggle and the relief field you enter. Non-resident PAYE uses the current flat 25% rule shown by GRA.

VAT scope

Standard VAT uses the post-reform structure effective January 1, 2026: 15% VAT, 2.5% NHIL, and 2.5% GETFund, all applied on the same taxable value.

Special cases

Bonuses, overtime, benefits in kind, sector-specific exemptions, and withholding scenarios may need separate treatment outside this tool.
