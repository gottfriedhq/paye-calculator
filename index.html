FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How is PAYE calculated in Ghana?

Resident PAYE is calculated on monthly chargeable income using graduated tax bands published by GRA. Non-resident individuals are generally taxed at a flat 25% of chargeable employment income.

What is the standard VAT rate in Ghana?

Under the VAT reforms effective January 1, 2026, Ghana's standard structure shown by GRA is 15% VAT plus 2.5% NHIL and 2.5% GETFund on the same taxable value.

How do Ghana treasury bills pay returns?

Treasury bills are discount instruments. You invest a lower amount today and receive the face value at maturity. Bank of Ghana commonly publishes both the discount rate and the equivalent interest rate.

Does the compound interest calculator support monthly savings?

Yes. It assumes monthly additions and estimates growth over the number of years you choose, while adjusting the monthly growth effect to match the compounding frequency selected.
